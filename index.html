Who is Michael Moran?

I am a student at Kansas State Polytechnic University in Salina studying Computer Systems Technology. This webpage actually began an assignment of mine. I have put a couple things about me on here. Let's begin then!

With the Formalities Out of the Way...

Here are some things about me. I grew up in Manhattan, Kansas always surrounded by the Kansas State Wildcats. I took an interest in computers in middle school, joining the coding club my school offered in 8th grade. In the summer after my 8th grade year, I got my first job. The first thing I did was save up enough money to build my very own PC, focusing the specs on gaming. By my freshman year of high school I had built my own computer and still enjoyed working on and with it throughout high school. Knowing that I wanted to work with computers as a career, I started looking at many colleges, eventually setting my eyes on Kansas State's Polytechnic University, where I am currently enrolled in the Computer Systems Technology major.
